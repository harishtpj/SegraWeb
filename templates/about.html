About Segra

Segra is a sustainability-driven platform designed to encourage eco-friendly behavior through transparent rewards and community collaboration.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable practices by rewarding positive environmental actions and fostering collaborative communities.

How It Works

  • Users earn EcoCoins through sustainable actions
  • Clubs coordinate collective efforts
  • All transactions are transparent and traceable

Why Segra?

Unlike traditional reward systems, Segra focuses on measurable impact, community trust, and long-term environmental value.

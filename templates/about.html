About Segra

Segra is a smart waste segregation system designed to promote responsible waste disposal using AI-powered classification and reward-based gamification.

Users dispose waste in a smart dustbin which automatically classifies the waste and directs it to the appropriate bin. After disposal, users are rewarded with EcoCoins that encourage sustainable behavior.

Segra also enables sustainability-focused clubs and organizations to conduct EcoDrives, allowing participants to contribute more while maintaining system integrity.

Developed as part of a college project to explore AI, IoT, and web technologies in building real-world sustainability solutions.
